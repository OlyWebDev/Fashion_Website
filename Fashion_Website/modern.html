Modern Fashion is all about wearing whatever you like. This is a great way to highlight your personality and make a statement. After many fashion trends, revolutions and styles, there is no inappropriate way of wearing anymore. Woman can wear one day a long skirt and the other day, she can wear a really shirt one, go out in a chic black dress or in the torn trendy jeans. All types of accessories are available too, from hats, to headbands, necklaces, bracelets, belts, fans, bags and etc. Shoes also have a variety of models, shapes and colors. There is a debate if fashion is a form of art. In my opinion it is and it's always been. Now that there is a lot of freedom around this topic, everyone can fit the outfit to her/his personality and enjoy self expression.

Nowadays, the overall look is all about being polished and natural. This is true for beauty products as well. The trend is to wear "no makeup makeup" look and a casual outfit. This beauty trend is also called an effortless look. Of course there is also a glam look that by the way can look "natural" too, but this kind of look usually goes with evening dresses, polished makeup, beautiful hair and prominent jewelry. It's so great to have such a versatile choice of self expression. It's like being a creator of your own style!
